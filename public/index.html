URL Shortener Microservice

This is a url shortener microservice

Example usage:

Create new short url

    /new/http://www.example.com

Response:

    {"shorturl": "https://url-shrt-zelite.herokuapp.com/4G",
    "longurl": "http://www.example.com"}

Follow short url

    https://url-shrt-zelite.herokuapp.com/4G"

Important Notice

This url shortener is a just a learning project. Please do not rely on the availability of this service. Also, I cannot take any responsibility for the destination of the URLs.
